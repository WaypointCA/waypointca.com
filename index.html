Federally required annual assessments for every healthcare covered entity. We identify gaps, build remediation plans, and produce evidence documentation that satisfies HHS Office for Civil Rights audit requirements.

Readiness evaluation for defense contractors preparing for third-party CMMC certification.

  • Remediation planning
  • Assessment preparation
